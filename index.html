Discover this beautiful county on foot

West Yorkshire is home to some of the most beautiful scenery that England has to offer, including the Pennines and the Peak District. It's valleys have a wealth of public footpaths allowing you to explore the stunning sights. There are woodlands, reservoirs, moorlands, rolling hills and dramatic uplands, and an abundance of wildlife! You can enjoy walking around West Yorkshire all year round - just make sure you have appropriate hiking gear!
